Mathematical Logic

How the deductions are calculated behind the scenes

Step 1: Extracting Base Price

To find the original base price before an 18% GST was added, we divide the total inclusive price by 1.18.

Base Price = Total Price ÷ 1.18

Step 2: Applying the Discount

The amount you choose to subtract is deducted directly from that extracted base price.

New Base Price = Base Price - Subtracted Amount

Step 3: Recalculating Final Price

Finally, we re-apply the 18% GST to the new base price to find the final total amount.

Final GST = New Base Price × 0.18

Final Price = New Base Price × 1.18

GST rate used: 18%  |  All values rounded to 2 decimal places
